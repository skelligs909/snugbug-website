## Key Takeaways

**For Canadian manufacturing:** Command Medical (Barrie, ON) and RK Manufacturing are your best bets for sterile device packaging on Canadian soil. Canada's medical device regulation through Health Canada is similar to FDA for Class I devices — no quality system certificate is required for Class I.

**For Irish/EU manufacturing:** Ensera (formerly SteriPack) is the strongest all-around option. They have the cleanroom infrastructure, sterile packing experience, ISO 13485 certification, and both EU and US regulatory compliance. Ireland is a global hub for medical devices — you'd be in good company.

**EU regulatory note:** If you plan to sell in Europe, you'll need CE marking under the EU Medical Device Regulation (MDR 2017/745). For Class I devices, this is a self-certification process (no Notified Body required), but you do need a European Authorized Representative and must register with the relevant national competent authority.
